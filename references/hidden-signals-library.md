## How to Use This Library

1. **Identify the prospect's industry** from research output or domain research.
2. **Scan the matching section** for signals that align with your research findings.
3. **Check adjacent industries** -- the best angles often come from cross-industry transfer.
4. **Use matched patterns as starting points**, not final angles. Adapt to the specific prospect's context.
5. **If no pattern matches**, that's a good sign -- you may have found a genuinely novel signal. Document it and add it to this library after testing.

## Contributing New Patterns

After testing an angle that achieves ALPHA status (8.0+ Asymmetry Score, >3% reply rate):
1. Add the signal pattern to the relevant industry section.
2. Include the actual reply rate and sample size in the Example Angle column.
3. Update the Shelf Life based on observed decay.
